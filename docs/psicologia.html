Small Steps, Big Changes

Psychology is useful in everyday life. Here are some immediate strategies you can use right now to center yourself.


Remember: Healing is non-linear. Some days you bloom, other days you rest. Both are necessary.

The 5-4-3-2-1 Technique

For anxiety: Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, and 1 you taste.

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Repeat until calm.

Journaling Prompts

Ask yourself: 'What is this emotion trying to tell me?' rather than trying to suppress it.
